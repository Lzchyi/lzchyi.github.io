Wanderly roadmap
Atmosphere with clearer intent.

This roadmap page gives Wanderly a calm planning surface for future work across place discovery, weather mood, and ambient listening.

App page Privacy policy
Current focus

Make each place feel believable

Weather, time, and sound should work together so Wanderly feels less like a utility and more like a quiet visit to a place.

Design principle

Calm before features

New ideas should deepen atmosphere and ease, not overwhelm the experience with dashboard-like complexity.

Roadmap lanes

Structured now, ready for us to tune together.

Now

Core atmosphere

  • Refine the main place view so weather, time, and ambient cues feel more unified.
  • Improve search and saved places so returning to favourite locations feels effortless.
  • Polish the visual mood transitions to keep the app calm and immersive.
Next

Depth without clutter

  • Explore richer ambient sound behaviour tied more closely to real conditions.
  • Strengthen favourites and revisit flows for people who use Wanderly as a quiet ritual.
  • Clarify lightweight place details without turning the app into a traditional weather dashboard.
Later

Longer-term shaping

  • Consider curated scenes or thematic journeys if they support the atmosphere-first identity.
  • Explore more nuanced sound and visual systems for repeat use.
  • Decide which discovery features deserve to become part of the permanent experience.
Planning note

A starting framework, not a locked plan

This roadmap page is intentionally light so we can shape the specific milestones together once we agree on how far Wanderly should lean into atmosphere, utility, and discovery.
