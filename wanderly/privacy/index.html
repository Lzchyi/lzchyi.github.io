Privacy Policy

Last updated: 03 April 2026

Overview

Wanderly is designed to be calm, simple, and private. This app does not require an account and does not collect personal data.

Location & Weather

When you search for a place, Wanderly uses Apple MapKit to provide suggestions. Selected locations are used to retrieve weather data via OpenWeather.

This information is used in real time and is not stored or tracked.

Data Storage

Wanderly stores limited data locally on your device, including selected locations and app preferences. This data is not transmitted to any external servers.

Audio

Wanderly plays ambient sounds based on weather conditions. No microphone access is used and no audio is recorded or transmitted.

Analytics & Tracking

Wanderly does not use analytics tools, tracking systems, or advertising services.

Third-Party Services

Wanderly uses Apple MapKit for location search and OpenWeather for weather data. These services may process data according to their own policies.
