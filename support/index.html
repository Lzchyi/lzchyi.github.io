What to include

So support can be faster.

1

App details

Tell us which app you are using and, if possible, the app version shown in the App Store or inside the app.

2

Device details

Include your iPhone or iPad model and iOS version so the issue can be reproduced more accurately.

3

Issue summary

Describe what happened, what you expected to happen, and the steps taken before the issue appeared.

Purchase help

For unlocks, restores, and billing-related questions.

In-app purchases

Restore purchases

If you already bought a feature and it does not appear unlocked, first try the app's Restore Purchases button. If the issue remains, email support with your app name and a short description.

Billing

Apple ID charges

All App Store billing is handled by Apple. If you need help with refunds or billing records, Apple Support may also be required depending on the issue.
