Primary support

Email

Contact hello@zhenchyi.com for all apps on this site.

  • Include the app name and version if available.
  • Include your iPhone, iPad, or Mac model and OS version for bug reports.
  • For purchase issues, include the App Store purchase date and whether Restore Purchases was tried.
Response

What support covers

Support covers bug reports, app usage questions, privacy questions, feedback, and purchase or restore-purchase help.

  • Replies are usually sent within 2 business days.
  • Apple handles App Store billing, refunds, and payment records.
  • Internal tool questions should identify the team or workspace involved.

Purchase help

App Store purchases are handled by Apple.

If a one-time Pro unlock does not appear, first use Restore Purchases inside the app. If it still fails, email support with the app name and a short description. Refunds and billing records may require Apple Support.
