Last updated: April 2026

Your Privacy Matters

ShotMark is built around a simple idea: your screenshots and notes are personal, and they should stay that way.

Data Storage

Screenshots, notes, voice recordings, and reminders are stored locally on your device. ShotMark does not upload your content to external servers.

Photo Library Access

Photo Library access is only used when you choose a screenshot to create a card. ShotMark does not modify or delete your photos.

Microphone Access

Microphone access is only used when you choose to record a voice note. These recordings are stored locally on your device.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, ShotMark schedules local notifications on your device. These are not remote push notifications.

No Analytics or Tracking

ShotMark does not include ads, tracking SDKs, or third-party analytics.

No Account Required

You can use ShotMark without creating an account or giving personal information.

Data Deletion

You can delete cards and recordings anytime inside the app. Deleting the app also removes its locally stored data.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact: hello@zhenchyi.com

TL;DR

Everything stays on your device. ShotMark does not collect, track, or upload your personal data.
