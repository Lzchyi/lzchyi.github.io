Y.E.S Hub Privacy Policy

Internal. Minimal. Respectful.

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Overview

Y.E.S Hub is designed for internal agency use and focuses on practical, private access to training and reference materials.

Accounts and Sign-In

When enabled, Y.E.S Hub may use Google Sign-In so the app can connect to an agency Google Drive location. Authentication is handled through Google's flow rather than custom credential storage inside the app.

Drive Content and Documents

The app may fetch document metadata and files from a connected shared storage location so users can browse, search, and open internal materials.

Local Storage and Offline Access

Bookmarks, recent items, profile details, and cached files may be stored on your device to support faster loading and offline access.

Analytics and Tracking

Y.E.S Hub is intended to stay free of ads and unnecessary third-party tracking, with the product focused on utility rather than marketing behavior.

Third-Party Services

If Y.E.S Hub connects to Google services or other linked storage providers, those services process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

TL;DR

Y.E.S Hub is built for internal agency reference use: local preferences, optional cached files, and only the account or storage access needed to open shared materials.
