RankHalo Privacy Policy

Clear. Local. Respectful.

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

RankHalo is designed to keep ranking tracking focused and private. The app does not require an account and keeps tracked data local to your device.

What RankHalo stores

Tracked apps, pinned markets, alerts, settings, and any optional profile name or photo used in the Home greeting are stored on your device.

Network connections

RankHalo connects to Apple's public App Store endpoints for rankings and app metadata, plus Apple StoreKit for purchases and restore.

Notifications

If you enable alerts, RankHalo schedules local notifications on your device. Notifications stay optional and can be turned off at any time.

Analytics and tracking

RankHalo does not include third-party ads, tracking SDKs, or third-party analytics in this app flow, and it does not sell your data.

Purchases

Any RankHalo Pro purchase is processed by Apple through StoreKit. The developer does not receive your payment card details.

Data deletion

You can remove tracked apps, clear settings, and disable notifications at any time. Deleting the app removes its locally stored RankHalo data from the device.

Tracked apps, pinned markets, alerts, and settings stay on your device, with Apple services used only for charts and purchases plus optional local notifications.
