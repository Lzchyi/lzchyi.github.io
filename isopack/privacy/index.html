Privacy Policy

Last updated: 03 April 2026

Overview

IsoPack is designed to respect your privacy. This app does not require an account and does not collect personal data.

Data Storage

All data created in IsoPack — including trips, packing items, lists, and settings — is stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to any external servers.

Photos

If you choose to add images to your trips or profile, IsoPack may request access to your photo library. This access is used only for selecting images and is never used for tracking or data collection.

Import & Export

IsoPack allows you to export and import trip data manually. These files are created and stored locally and are only shared when you choose to do so.

Analytics & Tracking

IsoPack does not use analytics tools, tracking systems, or third-party advertising services.

Third-Party Services

IsoPack does not integrate with external services that collect or process user data.
