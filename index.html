App ecosystem

Different categories, same philosophy. Every app lives under one simple home, so support, privacy, and product identity stay consistent.

One home

A central place for all apps, updates, privacy pages, and support links. Easy to maintain, easy to trust.

One design language

Minimal visuals, clean spacing, and a premium tone across every page — even simple legal pages should feel polished.

One growing portfolio

New apps can be added over time without redesigning the entire site. Just create a new folder and a new card.

Privacy & support

Each app should have its own privacy policy page. That keeps things clearer for users and safer for platform submission.

Per-app privacy policy

Use separate links such as /wanderly/privacy/, /packmate/privacy/, and /carsense/privacy/.

Support email

Replace the contact link with your real address, such as hello@zhenchyi.com.

App Store ready

Public, accessible, simple URLs help when submitting apps for review and make your indie brand feel more complete.
