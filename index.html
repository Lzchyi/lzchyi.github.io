Privacy language

Clear, local-first, and easy to scan.

ShotMark

On-device

Everything stays on your device. ShotMark does not collect, track, or upload your personal content.

Wanderly

Preferences only

Wanderly stays lightweight: no account, no ads, no microphone recording, and only the services needed to search places and fetch weather.

IsoPack

On-device

Your packing data stays local unless you explicitly export it yourself.

RankHalo

Local-first ranking data

Tracked apps, pinned markets, alerts, and settings stay on your device, with Apple services used only for charts and purchases plus optional on-device notifications.

CarSense

Planned local-first

CarSense is still in development, but the privacy direction is clear: keep it focused, local, and respectful.

Still13

On-device progress

Still13 is a local-first, calm game experience with no account and no tracking.
